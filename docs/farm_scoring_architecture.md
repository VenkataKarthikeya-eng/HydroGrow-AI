# HydroGrow AI Farm Scoring Architecture

Specification detailing the composite farm intelligence scoring algorithm across productivity, sustainability, automation, and crop health.

---

## 1. Scoring Formula & Factors

$$\text{Overall Score} = 0.35 \times \text{Productivity} + 0.25 \times \text{Health} + 0.20 \times \text{Automation} + 0.20 \times \text{Sustainability}$$

- **Productivity (35%):** Biomass accumulation vs. expected growth curve.
- **Crop Health (25%):** Computer vision leaf anomaly rate & pH/EC stability.
- **Automation (20%):** Closed-loop actuator uptime & setpoint compliance.
- **Sustainability (20%):** Water recycling efficiency & kWh per kg fresh weight.
