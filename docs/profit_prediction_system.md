# HydroGrow AI Profitability & Cost Analytics System

System documentation for production cost accounting, estimated revenue projection, and crop ROI ranking.

---

## 1. Profit Calculation Model

- **Direct Inputs:** Water nutrient salts, energy kWh (lighting + HVAC), seedling starting materials.
- **Fixed Allocation:** Channel depreciation & system maintenance amortized per crop cycle.
- **Net Profit Margin:**

$$\text{Profit Margin (\%)} = \frac{\text{Estimated Market Revenue} - \text{Total Production Cost}}{\text{Estimated Market Revenue}} \times 100$$
